Conferences

Professional Development and Networking

Conferences are a great way to develop yourself and to network with other people. In addition to a number of networking opportunities, conferences provide a great way to learn about other people's research, new topics within the scope of the conference, and hear several talks by distinguished speakers.

Mathematical Association of America Southeastern Section Meeting

The MAASE Section Meeting is one of the biggest conferences that is regularly attended by WCU Students. The MAASE Meeting is held a university in the MAA's southeastern region. Some previous universities hosting this conference include Clemson University, Macon University, and several others.
