About Us

We are an offical chapter of the ACM or Association for Computing Machinery, but welcome anyone who is ready to learn and have some fun. Our club is dedicated to providing useful resources and practical workshops to help in the development of mission critical skills that any person will need throughout their career. We have people from many majors and try to offer a diverse range of activites for all.
